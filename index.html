Higher Level Maths - Important Formulas with Examples

Your quick reference guide for Leaving Cert Higher Level Maths

Algebra

  • Expansion of a binomial square:
    \[ (a + b)^2 = a^2 + 2ab + b^2 \]
    Example: Expand \( (x + 3)^2 \)
    Answer: \( x^2 + 6x + 9 \)
  • Difference of squares:
    \[ a^2 - b^2 = (a - b)(a + b) \]
    Example: Factorise \( x^2 - 16 \)
    Answer: \( (x - 4)(x + 4) \)
  • Quadratic formula:
    \[ x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} \]
    Example: Solve \( 2x^2 + 3x - 2 = 0 \)
    Answer: \( x = 0.5 \) or \( x = -2 \)

Trigonometry

  • Pythagorean identity:
    \[ \sin^2 \theta + \cos^2 \theta = 1 \]
    Example: If \( \sin \theta = 0.6 \), find \( \cos \theta \)
    Answer: \( 0.8 \)
  • Sine addition formula:
    \[ \sin(A \pm B) = \sin A \cos B \pm \cos A \sin B \]
    Example: Calculate \( \sin 75^\circ \)
    Answer: \( \approx 0.9659 \)
  • Area of triangle:
    \[ \text{Area} = \frac{1}{2} ab \sin C \]
    Example: \( a=7, b=9, C=60^\circ \)
    Answer: \( 27.27 \)

Calculus

  • Derivative power rule:
    \[ \frac{d}{dx} x^n = n x^{n-1} \]
    Example: \( \frac{d}{dx} x^5 = 5x^4 \)
  • Integral power rule:
    \[ \int x^n \, dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + C \]
    Example: \( \int x^3 \, dx = \frac{x^4}{4} + C \)
  • Derivative of sine:
    \[ \frac{d}{dx} \sin x = \cos x \]
    Example: \( \frac{d}{dx} \sin x = \cos x \)

Coordinate Geometry

  • Slope (gradient):
    \[ m = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1} \]
    Example: Between \( (2, 3) \) and \( (5, 11) \): \( m = \frac{8}{3} \)
  • Equation of a line:
    \[ y - y_1 = m (x - x_1) \]
    Example: Through \( (2, 3) \) with slope \( \frac{8}{3} \)
    \( y - 3 = \frac{8}{3}(x - 2) \)
  • Distance formula:
    \[ d = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2} \]
    Example: Between \( (2, 3) \) and \( (5, 11) \): \( d \approx 8.54 \)

More Important Formulas

  • Equation of a circle:
    \[ (x - h)^2 + (y - k)^2 = r^2 \]
    Circle centred at \( (3, -2) \) with radius 5:
    \( (x - 3)^2 + (y + 2)^2 = 25 \)
  • Sum of an arithmetic series:
    \[ S_n = \frac{n}{2} (2a + (n-1)d) \]
    First term \( a=3 \), common difference \( d=2 \), sum of first 5 terms:
    \( S_5 = 35 \)
  • Sum of a geometric series:
    \[ S_n = a \frac{1 - r^n}{1 - r} \quad (r \neq 1) \]
    \( a=2 \), \( r=3 \), sum of first 4 terms:
    \( S_4 = 80 \)
  • Logarithm definition:
    \[ y = \log_b x \quad \Longleftrightarrow \quad b^y = x \]
    \( \log_2 8 = 3 \)
  • Change of base formula:
    \[ \log_b a = \frac{\log_c a}{\log_c b} \]
    Calculate \( \log_2 10 \) using natural logs:
    \( \approx 3.32 \)
  • Vector magnitude:
    \[ |\mathbf{v}| = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2} \]
    Vector \( \mathbf{v} = (3, 4) \): magnitude = 5
  • Dot product:
    \[ \mathbf{a} \cdot \mathbf{b} = a_x b_x + a_y b_y \]
    \( \mathbf{a} = (1, 2), \mathbf{b} = (3, 4) \)
    \( = 11 \)
  • Circle arc length:
    \[ s = r \theta \] (where \( \theta \) in radians)
    \( r=5 \), \( \theta = \frac{\pi}{3} \): \( s \approx 5.24 \)
  • Circle sector area:
    \[ A = \frac{1}{2} r^2 \theta \] (where \( \theta \) in radians)
    \( r=5 \), \( \theta = \frac{\pi}{3} \): \( A \approx 13.09 \)
  • Binomial theorem (first 3 terms):
    \[ (a + b)^n \approx a^n + n a^{n-1} b + \frac{n(n-1)}{2} a^{n-2} b^2 + \dots \]
    \( (x + 2)^3 = x^3 + 6x^2 + 12x + 8 \)

Area & Perimeter Formulas

  • Area of rectangle:
    \[ A = lw \]
    Length \( l=5 \), width \( w=3 \): \( A = 15 \)
  • Perimeter of rectangle:
    \[ P = 2(l + w) \]
    Length \( l=5 \), width \( w=3 \): \( P = 16 \)
  • Area of square:
    \[ A = s^2 \]
    Side \( s=4 \): \( A = 16 \)
  • Perimeter of square:
    \[ P = 4s \]
    Side \( s=4 \): \( P = 16 \)
  • Area of triangle:
    \[ A = \frac{1}{2} b h \]
    Base \( b=6 \), height \( h=4 \): \( A = 12 \)
  • Perimeter of triangle:
    \[ P = a + b + c \]
    Sides \( 3, 4, 5 \): \( P = 12 \)
  • Area of parallelogram:
    \[ A = b h \]
    Base \( b=7 \), height \( h=3 \): \( A = 21 \)
  • Perimeter of parallelogram:
    \[ P = 2(a + b) \]
    Sides \( a=5 \), \( b=7 \): \( P = 24 \)
  • Area of trapezium:
    \[ A = \frac{1}{2} (a + b) h \]
    Parallel sides \( a=4 \), \( b=6 \), height \( h=3 \): \( A = 15 \)
  • Perimeter of trapezium:
    \[ P = a + b + c + d \]
    Sides \( 4, 6, 3, 5 \): \( P = 18 \)
  • Area of circle:
    \[ A = \pi r^2 \]
    Radius \( r=3 \): \( A \approx 28.27 \)
  • Circumference of circle:
    \[ C = 2 \pi r \]
    Radius \( r=3 \): \( C \approx 18.85 \)

Volume Formulas

  • Volume of cube:
    \[ V = s^3 \]
    Side \( s = 3 \): \( V = 27 \)
  • Volume of cuboid:
    \[ V = l \times w \times h \]
    \( l = 4, w = 3, h = 2 \): \( V = 24 \)
  • Volume of cylinder:
    \[ V = \pi r^2 h \]
    \( r = 2, h = 5 \): \( V \approx 62.83 \)
  • Volume of cone:
    \[ V = \frac{1}{3} \pi r^2 h \]
    \( r = 2, h = 6 \): \( V \approx 25.13 \)
  • Volume of sphere:
    \[ V = \frac{4}{3} \pi r^3 \]
    \( r = 3 \): \( V \approx 113.1 \)
  • Volume of hemisphere:
    \[ V = \frac{2}{3} \pi r^3 \]
    \( r = 3 \): \( V \approx 56.55 \)
